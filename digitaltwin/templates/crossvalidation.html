Case study: three-storey structure

In this page, the experimental and numerical data collected from the numerical and physical prototype of the three-story building in each university are presented.
Four prototypes of the three-storey structure were made in four different universities: Sheffeild, Swansea, Southampton and Bristol university. In addition, for each prototype,
a numerical model was performed for validation purpose.
A validation between the numerical and experimental data is shown in addition to a comparision of experimental results collected from the different prototypes of the physical stucture.
